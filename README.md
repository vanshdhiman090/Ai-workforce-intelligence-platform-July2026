# AI Workforce Intelligence Platform

## Problem
HR and workforce planning teams need to know which skills are in demand 
now and emerging next, but today that requires manual research and 
analyst time — which is slow, expensive, and often outdated by the time 
it's delivered. This leads to reactive hiring and training decisions 
instead of proactive ones.
